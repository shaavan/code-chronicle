# Log 377: Where It Can Live

> 05 May 2026

## What I worked on

- [Continued] the BOLT 12 recurrence work on the `rust-recurrence` branch by
  implementing the invoice-side half of the speculative continuation-token
  proposal.
- Added `invoice_recurrence_token` to recurring offer invoices as a provisional
  odd TLV and threaded it through builders, accessors, parsing, and
  serialization.
- Kept the field tightly bounded by rejecting empty tokens and disallowing it
  on non-recurring offer invoices, refund invoices, and static invoices instead
  of letting it quietly widen invoice semantics.
- Documented throughout the code that the token remains speculative and is not
  part of the upstream recurrence PR, so the invoice support does not present
  itself as more settled than it is.
- Verified the work with a full local workspace test run and added focused
  coverage for token round-tripping on recurring invoices while rejecting the
  invalid construction paths.

## Reflection

Today looked like invoice-side plumbing at first, but most of the real work was
deciding where that plumbing was allowed to stop.

Adding the token field itself was straightforward enough once the request-side
piece already existed. The harder part was keeping the implementation from
smuggling in a broader claim about invoices. A continuation token only makes
sense inside one narrow recurrence path, and the code needed to say that
plainly. That is why the useful decisions were all negative ones: empty values
do not count, non-recurring offers do not get to carry it, refunds do not get
to reuse it, and static invoices do not quietly inherit a field that belongs to
some other conversation.

I liked that this kept the speculative part visible instead of trying to make
it feel natural through repetition. The token now exists on the invoice side,
but it still reads like a proposal under test rather than an accepted extension
of BOLT 12. That made the day feel steadier than a simple feature-add. I was no
longer just filling in the other half of the round trip. I was making sure the
new surface only exists in the places where I can still defend what it means.
