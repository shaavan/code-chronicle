## Reflection

Today was about writing the final missing piece of the dummy hop follow-up.

The goal was to introduce the check that ensures a payment fails if there is not
enough amount or CLTV delta to account for the fees and expiry introduced by
dummy hops. In other words, dummy hops should behave exactly like real hops in
the route, and payments that cannot satisfy their requirements must fail
accordingly.

I made good progress toward this by laying out the general structure of the test
that exercises this behavior. While the overall shape is now in place, there are
still a few rough edges to clean up before everything is fully working.

That will be the first thing to tackle as the new week begins.
