> 06 April 2026

## What I worked on

- [Continued] the currency conversion work.
- Restructured `InvoiceRequest` amount semantics and updated APIs
  (`amount_msats`, `payable_amount_msats`).
- Replayed and cleaned early currency-conversion commits on the new foundation.

## Reflection

Today was about resetting the foundation and building back on it cleanly.

I separated the meaning of amounts in `InvoiceRequest`. `amount_msats()` now
reflects only what was explicitly requested, while `payable_amount_msats(...)`
represents the effective payable amount. That clarifies intent and removes
ambiguity that had built up over time.

With that in place, I replayed the currency-conversion work on top of this
cleaner base. The commits now read more naturally, and the flow is easier to
follow from start to finish.

I also simplified the public API surface so conversion-aware behavior lives in
the main methods instead of being spread across wrappers.

By the end, the structure feels more deliberate. The base is clearer, and the
rest of the work sits on it more cleanly.

A good day of getting the ground right.
